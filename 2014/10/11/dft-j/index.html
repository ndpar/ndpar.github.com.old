Descrete Fourier Transform in J

Oct 11th, 2014

The Fourier analysis in general and the Fourier transform in particular has always been one of my favourite topics in mathematics. When I started learning the J programming language I thought that implementing the descrete Fourier transform (DFT) in J must be really elegant. One rainy weekend I finally found time to do that, and this post is a result of my experiments.
